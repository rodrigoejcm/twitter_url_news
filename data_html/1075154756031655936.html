Drunk teacher bit 14-year-old girl’s backside in pool then tried to bribe cops

Jacob GeanousMonday 17 Dec 2018 8:28 pm
Share this article via facebookShare this article via twitterShare this article via messenger
Share this with

A middle school teacher has admitted biting a 14-year-old girl’s butt in a pool while drunk before trying to bribe cops not to arrest him.

Jonathan Herbert, 30, had been drinking at Lake Lanier in Georgia before he swam underwater and bit the girl’s backside at a Fourth of July celebration in front of multiple beachgoers.

When police confronted Herbert about the incident, Herbert attempted to offer officers a $200 bribe to let him walk away.

Police said the Herbert did not have any connection to the girl or her family.

On August 1, he resigned from his teaching job at Gwinnet County Public Schools.

Herbert entered a first offender plea in court on Friday, because he did not have any past convictions, to charges of battery, public drunkenness, and bribery.

He was also charged with sexual battery against a child under 16 and second-degree cruelty of children, but prosecutor’s dropped those charges because ‘the sexual intent behind the sexual battery and the evidence is very different than other sexual assault cases’

Herbert was sentenced to 30 days in jail and four years of probation.

As a part of his first offender plea, all charges will be wiped from his record if he successfully completes his sentence.

The victim and her family agreed to the plea deal because they did not want the girl to be forced to testify.
