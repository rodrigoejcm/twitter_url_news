    Holly Willoughby Instagram: Star shares saucy snap amid pal Fearne Cotton's HUGE decision

    HOLLY WILLOUGHBY has this morning reacted to Fearne Cotton’s major announcement on Instagram, sharing a saucy snap of the pair on the set of Celebrity Juice.

    By Jessica Earnshaw
    PUBLISHED: 08:27, Tue, Dec 18, 2018 | UPDATED: 09:23, Tue, Dec 18, 2018
    0

    Holly Willoughby took to Instagram with a moving message in view of her 4.8 million followers, saying she was going to miss best friend and co-star Fearne Cotton. The This Morning favourite’s post comes after the presenter quit as a Celebrity Juice panellist after 10 years. The ITV2 gameshow sees both the I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! star and her fellow presenter, both 37, go head-to-head alongside teammates in a variety naughty and wacky challenges. The mother-of-three remembered one particular game which saw the pair stood with a banana in their mouths.

    I’m going to miss you being on the other side of my banana, Fearne Cotton

    Holly Willoughby

    Holly captioned it: “I’m going to miss you being on the other side of my banana… thank you for 10 years of laughter.

    “Love you @fearnecotton… we are going to MISS you on @celebjuiceofficial SOOO much xxxx.”(sic)

    She was soon inundated with messages from fans, with one user replying: “Oh noooo, won’t b the same without u, good luck in whatever your going to do x.”(sic)

    Two others continued: “Still can’t believe this,” and: “How about a show with both of you?”

    Celebrity Juice host Keith Lemon - real name Leigh Francis - also said it was the "end of an era" as he thanked Fearne on the picture-sharing app.

    Alongside a montage of pictures of the pair together, the 45-year-old stated: "End of an era!

    Holly Willoughby Instagram: The star has spoken out after Fearne Cotton quit Celebrity Juice (Image: ITV • GETTY • INSTAGRAM/HOLLY WILLOUGHBY)

    Holly Willoughby Instagram: Fearne Cotton and her Celebrity Juice co-star were pictured on set (Image: INSTAGRAM/HOLLY WILLOUGHBY • ITV)

    Holly Willoughby Instagram: Fearne Cotton, Keith Lemon and the star on Celebrity Juice (Image: ITV)

    “Gonna miss @fearnecotton Fanks for the fun times. Love you deeply! Good luck with your next chapter!”

    He signed off: “I hope one day we'll play again. But the show must go on. Me and Holly and a new captain will be back in March for more @celebjuiceofficial.”

    Fearne decided to call it a day, revealing the news yesterday, to focus on other projects.

    She told The Sun: "After 10 years of complete fun, I have decided to leave Celebrity Juice.

    "It has been a joy to be part of this magnificent show and I wish the team the very best in future series."

    Holly Willoughby Instagram: Fearne Cotton was also thanked by Celebrity Juice host Keith Lemon (Image: INSTAGRAM/KEITH LEMON)

    Fearne has appeared in 18 out of 20 series, only taking a break in 2013 when she was on maternity leave and was replaced by Kelly Brook.

    In October, the mother-of-two said she was too busy to have a social life, in an interview with Women's Health magazine.

    She said: "I used to see my friends every day, but I just can't fit it in right now. If I did I'd be burnt out because my work life is so busy.

    "So, I think you do have to lose something. My girlfriends aren't going anywhere."

    Celebrity Juice continues Thursday at 10pm on ITV2.
