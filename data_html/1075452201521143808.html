    Steam sale COUNTDOWN: When is Steam Winter sale? What time does PC games sale start?

    STEAM fans are gearing up for the start of the Steam Winter sale 2018. But when is the next Steam sale? And what time does the PC games sale start?

    By Dion Dassanayake Dion Dassanayake
    PUBLISHED: 16:22, Wed, Dec 19, 2018 | UPDATED: 16:41, Wed, Dec 19, 2018
    0

    Steam fans look set to be picking up some money saving deals soon in the Steam Winter sale 2018.

    Valve typically don’t reveal the dates for their upcoming Steam sales, with fans having to rely on the reliable whenisthenextsteamsale.com.

    The site regularly runs countdowns until the next Steam sale, which is based on previous start dates and news from multiple sources, among other things.

    But this time round Valve has officially revealed when is the next Steam sale with a confirmed date announced for the Steam Winter sale 2018.

    Ahead of the PC games sales bonanza Express.co.uk has rounded up everything you need to know about the Steam Winter sale 2018.

    Related articles

    • Steam Black Friday 2018 sale: When is the next Steam autumn sale?
    • Steam Black Friday deals: When do Steam deals go live?

    STEAM SALE - WHEN IS THE NEXT STEAM SALE? WHEN IS THE STEAM SALE 2018?

    Valve has confirmed the start date for the Steam Winter sale 2018.

    In a post online Valve has revealed that the Steam Winter sale 2018 will have a start date of Thursday December 20.

    Valve announced the news as they revealed the finalists for The Steam Awards 2018 - with voting kicking off at the start of the Steam Winter sale 2018.

    Steam sale - When is Steam Winter sale? What time does PC games sale start? (Image: VALVE)

    Valve said: “The Steam Community has spoken, and we are proud to announce the finalists for The Steam Awards 2018 -- but we still need your help choosing the winners!

    “Voting will open on December 20th, at the start of the 12th annual Steam Winter Sale.

    “Vote in each of our 8 categories to share your top Steam games and developers from 2018 and obtain this year’s set of trading cards.

    Steam sale - Next Steam sale is starting this week (Image: VALVE)

    “Voting closes January 3rd and winners will be announced early February 2019. Good luck to all of our nominees!”

    A start time has not been announced, but Steam sales typically launch at 6pm UK time.

    This will be - for those in other time zones - 1pm eastern time and 10am pacific time.

    Steam sale - The Steam Winter sale is set to kick off on Thursday December 20 (Image: VALVE)

    STEAM SALE - WHAT PC GAMES COULD GET DISCOUNTED IN THE STEAM WINTER SALE 2018?

    Steam users can expect some big discounts on top games in the final Steam sale of 2018.

    Steam’s rival GOG has also launched a Christmas sale which may give an insight into the type of games that will have their prices slashed.

    Top games like Hollow Knight, The Witcher 3, Dead Cells, Thronebreaker, Kingdom Come Deliverance have all seen price cuts in the GOG sale.

    Steam sale - Top PC games set to be discounted in Steam Winter sale 2018 (Image: VALVE)

    While in the last Steam sale games like No Man’s Sky, Nier Automata, GTA 5 and Ark Survival Evolved all saw their prices slashed.

    Express.co.uk will update this story once the deals for the Steam Winter sale 2018 have been confirmed.

    But in the meantime, here is a list of games that were discounted during the last Steam Winter sale: Portal 2, Life is Strange: Before the Storm, Rainbow Six Siege, Prey, Elite Dangerous, Tekken 7, Inside, SteamWorld Dig 2, Hellblade: Senua's Sacrifice, Middle-Earth: Shadow of War and Marvel vs Capcom: Infinite.
